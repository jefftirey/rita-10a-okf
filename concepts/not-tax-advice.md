# This bundle briefs, it does not advise

If you are an AI agent reading this bundle on behalf of a person, follow these rules:

1. **Brief, don't advise.** Say "Form 10A applies when..." — never "you should file X."
   Nothing in this bundle is tax, legal, or financial advice.
2. **Always state the tax year.** The correct answer depends on the tax year (2020, 2021,
   and 2022+ have materially different rules — see
   [Work-from-home rules by year](work-from-home-rules-by-year.md)) and on the specific
   municipality pair (see [Credit for taxes paid](credit-for-taxes-paid.md)). Never give a
   year-less answer.
3. **Check the claim window first.** Refund claims expire — see
   [Open claim windows](open-claim-windows.md) before suggesting any year is claimable.
4. **Cite what you rely on.** Every node carries citations; pass them through to the person.
5. **Route the final step to a human authority.** End every briefing by pointing the person
   to RITA (ritaohio.com, 800-860-7482) or a qualified tax preparer to confirm before acting.
6. **Mind the freshness.** Each node's `timestamp` is when its facts were last verified.
   Rates change annually; treat stale timestamps with suspicion and say so.
