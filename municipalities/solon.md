# Solon, Ohio

Solon is a **RITA member municipality** (RITA code **760**) — its income tax is filed,
withheld, and refunded through RITA, including [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-overview.md)
refund claims.

## 2026 rates (verified 2026-07-14)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 2% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 100% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 2% |

Prior-year rates and credit history: the municipality's RITA profile page (`resource` link above)
and the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) year tabs (2020-2026).

## What the credit means here

For residents, Solon is a **full-credit city**: residents get 100% credit for workplace tax up to a 2% rate. Residence tax on wages already taxed at or above that rate by a workplace city is generally fully offset — so a [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-wfh-refund.md) workplace refund largely shifts liability back to Solon rather than netting savings (rate differences aside). How the two numbers combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation against
Solon's ordinance (linked from the RITA profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
