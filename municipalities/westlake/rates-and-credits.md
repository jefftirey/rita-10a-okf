# Westlake rates and credits (2026)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 1.5% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 100% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 1.5% |

**These are tax year 2026 values**, verified 2026-07-14. For a refund claim, use the claim
year's values: the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) has year tabs
back to 2020, and the "Recent Tax and Credit History" on Westlake's
[RITA profile page](https://www.ritaohio.com/Municipalities/Home/MemberPage?id=840) shows
effective dates.

For residents, Westlake is a **full-credit city**: residents get 100% credit for workplace tax up to a 1.5% rate, so residence tax on wages already taxed at or above that rate elsewhere is generally fully offset. A workplace refund therefore largely shifts liability back to Westlake rather than netting savings (rate differences aside). How the factor and limit combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation
against Westlake's ordinance (on the profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
