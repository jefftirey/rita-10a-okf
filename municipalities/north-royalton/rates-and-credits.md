# North Royalton rates and credits (2026)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 2% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 100% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 1.25% |

**These are tax year 2026 values**, verified 2026-07-14. For a refund claim, use the claim
year's values: the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) has year tabs
back to 2020, and the "Recent Tax and Credit History" on North Royalton's
[RITA profile page](https://www.ritaohio.com/Municipalities/Home/MemberPage?id=570) shows
effective dates.

For residents, North Royalton is a **capped-credit city**: the 100% credit factor applies only to workplace tax up to a 1.25% rate — below North Royalton's own 2% — so residents working in higher-rate cities owe North Royalton the remainder. A workplace refund produces partial real savings; run the math. How the factor and limit combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation
against North Royalton's ordinance (on the profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
