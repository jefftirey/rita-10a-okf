# Berea, Ohio

Berea is a **RITA member municipality** (RITA code **100**) — its income tax is filed,
withheld, and refunded through RITA, including [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-overview.md)
refund claims.

## 2026 rates (verified 2026-07-14)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 2% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 100% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 1.5% |

Prior-year rates and credit history: the municipality's RITA profile page (`resource` link above)
and the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) year tabs (2020-2026).

## What the credit means here

For residents, Berea is a **capped-credit city**: the 100% credit factor applies only to workplace tax up to a 1.5% rate, below Berea's own 2% — residents working in higher-rate cities owe Berea the remainder. A [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-wfh-refund.md) workplace refund produces partial real savings; run the math. How the two numbers combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation against
Berea's ordinance (linked from the RITA profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
