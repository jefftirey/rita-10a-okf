# Broadview Heights, Ohio

Broadview Heights is a **RITA member municipality** (RITA code **140**) — its income tax is filed,
withheld, and refunded through RITA, including [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-overview.md)
refund claims.

## 2026 rates (verified 2026-07-14)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 2% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 75% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 2% |

Prior-year rates and credit history: the municipality's RITA profile page (`resource` link above)
and the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) year tabs (2020-2026).

## What the credit means here

For residents, Broadview Heights is a **partial-credit city**: only 75% of workplace tax is credited (counted up to a 2% rate), so residents largely pay both cities on the same wages. A legitimate [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-wfh-refund.md) workplace refund here is mostly real net savings — this is the profile where WFH claims matter most. How the two numbers combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation against
Broadview Heights's ordinance (linked from the RITA profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
