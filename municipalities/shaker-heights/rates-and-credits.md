# Shaker Heights rates and credits (2026)

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 2.25% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 50% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 1% |

**These are tax year 2026 values**, verified 2026-07-14. For a refund claim, use the claim
year's values: the [RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) has year tabs
back to 2020, and the "Recent Tax and Credit History" on Shaker Heights's
[RITA profile page](https://www.ritaohio.com/Municipalities/Home/MemberPage?id=750) shows
effective dates.

For residents, Shaker Heights is a **partial-credit city**: only 50% of workplace tax is credited (counted up to a 1% rate), so residents largely pay both cities on the same wages. A legitimate workplace refund here is mostly real net savings — the profile where WFH claims matter most. How the factor and limit combine is explained in
[Credit for taxes paid](../../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md); confirm exact computation
against Shaker Heights's ordinance (on the profile page) or RITA's Form 37 instructions.
