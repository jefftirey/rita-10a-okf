# Official documents and where to find them

This bundle summarizes; **these are the real documents**. A consuming agent should hand the
person the actual form and the actual rule, not a paraphrase. All links verified 2026-07-14;
RITA's direct PDF URLs occasionally change, so if one 404s, start from the forms index.

## The form

- **Forms index (all years, always current):** https://www.ritaohio.com/individuals/home/formdownloads
- Form 10A, tax year 2025: https://www.ritaohio.com/Media/703127/Form10A%202025%20FINAL.pdf
- Form 10A instructions, tax year 2025: https://cdn.ritaohio.com/Media/703124/2025%20Form%2010A%20FINAL%20Instructions.pdf
- Form 10A, tax year 2024: https://cdn.ritaohio.com/Media/703143/Form10A%202024v2.pdf
  (instructions: https://cdn.ritaohio.com/Media/703154/10A%20Updated%20instructions.pdf)
- Form 10A, tax year 2023: https://cdn.ritaohio.com/Media/703144/Form10A%202023v2.pdf
- **Mail completed forms to:** Regional Income Tax Agency, PO Box 95422, Cleveland, OH 44101-0033
  (paper only — no email, e-file, or fax).

## RITA's rules and help

- Refunds page (process, 90-day clock): https://www.ritaohio.com/Individuals/Home/Refunds
- Refunds FAQ: https://www.ritaohio.com/Individuals/Faqs?category=I&subcategory=Refunds
- Income Tax Rules & Regulations (eff. 2016, amended 2025-01-01): https://cdn.ritaohio.com/Media/702857/RREGS_EFF%20JAN%201%202016_revised%20012225_061616.pdf
- Tax Rates Table (every member's rate/credit, year tabs 2020–2026): https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable
- Member municipality list (each links a profile with ordinances): https://www.ritaohio.com/municipalities
- MyAccount (status checking) and phone: https://www.ritaohio.com · 800-860-7482

## The law

- ORC 718.011 (20-day rule): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.011
- ORC 718.03 (withholding): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.03
- ORC 718.12 (limitations, interest): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.12
- ORC 718.19 (refunds, 3-year window): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.19
- Schaad v. Alder, 2024-Ohio-525 (slip opinion): https://www.supremecourt.ohio.gov/rod/docs/pdf/0/2024/2024-ohio-525.pdf

## Adjacent agencies

- CCA (Cleveland and other CCA members — not RITA): https://www.ccaohio.gov/member-municipalities
- Ohio Dept. of Taxation "The Finder" (rate lookup by address, incl. JEDDs): https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov
