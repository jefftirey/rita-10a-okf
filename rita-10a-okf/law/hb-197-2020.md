# HB 197 (2020), Section 29

Am. Sub. HB 197, 133rd General Assembly (Ohio's omnibus COVID-19 emergency bill), signed
2020-03-27. Uncodified **Section 29** (enrolled bill, pp. 340–341), verbatim:

> "Notwithstanding section 718.011 of the Revised Code, and for the purposes of Chapter 718. of
> the Revised Code, during the period of the emergency declared by Executive Order 2020-01D,
> issued on March 9, 2020, and for thirty days after the conclusion of that period, any day on
> which an employee performs personal services at a location, including the employee's home, to
> which the employee is required to report for employment duties because of the declaration
> shall be deemed to be a day performing personal services at the employee's principal place of
> work."

**Dates of effect** (per the Legislative Service Commission's HB 110 analysis, p. 446 and
fn. 133): keyed to the emergency declared 2020-03-09 (Executive Order 2020-01D); the emergency
was rescinded 2021-06-18 (Executive Order 2021-08D); *"The temporary rule expired on
July 18, 2021."* Section 29 was subsequently amended by [HB 110 §610.115](hb-110-2021.md).

Constitutionality was litigated; the Ohio Supreme Court's decision is
[Schaad v. Alder](schaad-v-alder.md).
