# Schaad v. Alder, 2024-Ohio-525

*Schaad v. Alder*, Slip Opinion No. **2024-Ohio-525**, 176 Ohio St.3d 158; Supreme Court of
Ohio; case no. 2022-0316; decided **February 14, 2024**; vote **5-2** (opinion by Justice
DeWine; Donnelly, Stewart, Brunner, and Deters, JJ., concurring; Chief Justice Kennedy and
Justice Fischer dissenting separately). **Read the opinion itself** (the `resource` link above);
the court's own words:

**Syllabus/headnote**, verbatim:

> "Temporary state law that directed municipalities where an employee's principal place of work
> was located to collect municipal income tax from the employee when the employee performed work
> outside that municipality does not violate state or federal Constitutions."

**Conclusion, ¶56**, verbatim:

> "Section 29 of H.B. 197 does not violate the Due Process Clause of the Fourteenth Amendment to
> the United States Constitution, and was a valid exercise of th[e General Assembly's]
> constitutional authority. We affirm the decision of the First District."

The law at issue: [HB 197 (2020), Section 29](hb-197-2020.md).
