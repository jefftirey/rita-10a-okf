# ORC Chapter 718: Municipal Income Tax

Ohio Revised Code Chapter 718 is the statewide framework for municipal income tax. **Read the
statute itself** — the links below are the law; this node locates the sections and quotes key
operative language verbatim, nothing more.

- **ORC 718.011 — "Occasional entrant exemption"** (current version effective 2015-03-23,
  HB 5, 130th GA): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.011
  Defines "principal place of work" at (A)(7): *"the fixed location to which an employee is
  required to report for employment duties on a regular and ordinary basis."*
- **ORC 718.03 — "Withholding taxes from qualifying wages"** (effective 2016-09-14, SB 172,
  131st GA): https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.03
- **ORC 718.12 — "Limitations"** (effective 2025-09-30, HB 96, 136th GA):
  https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.12
  Division (C): *"A claim for a refund of municipal income taxes shall be brought within the
  time limitation provided in section 718.19 of the Revised Code."*
- **ORC 718.19 — "Requests for refunds"** (effective 2025-09-30, HB 96, 136th GA):
  https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-718.19
  Division (B)(1): *"requests for refund shall be filed with the tax administrator, on the form
  prescribed by the tax administrator within three years after the tax return, including any
  valid extension, was due or paid, whichever is later."*

For tax years 2020–2021, uncodified session law modified how these sections applied to remote
work: [HB 197 §29](hb-197-2020.md) and [HB 110](hb-110-2021.md).
