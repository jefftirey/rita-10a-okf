# Hybrid worker, 2024: Medina resident, Solon office

**Fact pattern.** An employee lives in [Medina](../municipalities/medina/index.md) and is
employed at an office in [Solon](../municipalities/solon/index.md). Through tax year 2024 the
employee worked a hybrid schedule (some days at the office, some at home in Medina), and the
employer withheld Solon tax on all wages.

**What applies** — each point is a pointer, not a conclusion; outcomes depend on the person's
actual facts and the municipalities' ordinances:

- **Tax year rules:** 2024 is governed by ordinary [ORC 718](../law/orc-718.md) — see
  [Work-from-home rules by year](../concepts/work-from-home-rules-by-year.md).
- **Claim window:** per [ORC 718.19(B)(1)](../law/orc-718.md), a 2024 refund request runs three
  years from the return due date — see [Open claim windows](../concepts/open-claim-windows.md).
- **The claim itself:** RITA [Form 10A](../procedures/form-10a-overview.md), claim reason 2
  ("Days Worked From Home"), with the form's own allocation worksheet and log of days out —
  see the [WFH procedure](../procedures/form-10a-wfh-refund.md) and
  [documentation checklist](../procedures/documentation-checklist.md). RITA's Rules &
  Regulations §9(F) state days-out refunds from a workplace municipality are available to
  non-residents of it.
- **The residence side:** RITA's instructions state residence returns "will be amended to
  disallow any residence tax credit taken for amounts refunded with this Form 10A" — see
  [Amended returns](../procedures/amended-returns.md). What the net outcome is for this person
  depends on Medina's credit (its published
  [rates and credits](../municipalities/medina/rates-and-credits.md), its ordinance, and RITA's
  Form 37 instructions) — this bundle does not compute it.
- **The documents:** [Official documents](../procedures/official-documents.md) links the actual
  Form 10A for 2024, its instructions, and the rates table.

**Resolution:** the numbers and the filing decision belong with RITA (800-860-7482) or a tax
preparer — see [This bundle briefs, it does not advise](../concepts/not-tax-advice.md).
