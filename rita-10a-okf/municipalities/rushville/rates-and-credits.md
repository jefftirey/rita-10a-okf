# Rushville rates and credits (2026)

As published in RITA's Tax Rates Table for tax year 2026 (retrieved 2026-07-14):

| Fact | Value |
|---|---|
| Municipal income tax rate | 1.000% |
| Credit factor (tax credit) | 0.000% |
| Credit rate (credit limit) | 0.000% |

**These are tax year 2026 values.** For a refund claim, use the claim year's values — the
[RITA Tax Rates Table](https://www.ritaohio.com/TaxRatesTable) has year tabs back to 2020, and
Rushville's [RITA profile page](https://www.ritaohio.com/Municipalities/Home/MemberPage?id=696)
shows its rate and credit history with effective dates.

How the credit factor and credit limit combine is defined by Rushville's own ordinance (on the
profile page) and applied through RITA's Form 37 and its instructions — see
[Credit for taxes paid](../../concepts/credit-for-taxes-paid.md). This bundle does not compute
credits.
