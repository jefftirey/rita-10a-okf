# ORC Chapter 718: Municipal Income Tax

Ohio Revised Code Chapter 718 sets the uniform statewide rules every municipal income tax must
follow (substantially rewritten by HB 5, 130th GA, effective 2016). The sections this bundle
relies on:

## 718.011 — Occasional entrant exemption (the 20-day rule)

No employer withholding for a municipality where the employee worked ≤ 20 days that year, with
exceptions for the principal place of work, presumed worksites, and residence. Defines
**"principal place of work"** at (A)(7): "the fixed location to which an employee is required to
report for employment duties on a regular and ordinary basis." Details:
[20-day occasional entrant rule](../concepts/twenty-day-occasional-entrant.md).
*Current version effective 2015-03-23.*

## 718.03 — Employer withholding

Withholding attaches to qualifying wages "earned by the employee in the municipal corporation" —
i.e., where work is physically performed — multiplied by that municipality's rate. Employers may
voluntarily withhold residence-city tax on request. *Current version effective 2016-09-14.*

## 718.19 — Requests for refunds

Refunds of overpayments **greater than $10**; requests must be filed "within three years after
the tax return, including any valid extension, was due or paid, whichever is later" ((B)(1));
denials are issued as appealable assessments ((B)(2)). Details:
[Statute of limitations](../concepts/statute-of-limitations.md).
*Current version effective 2025-09-30 (HB 96, 136th GA).*

## 718.12 — Limitations and interest

(C): refund claims follow 718.19's clock. (D): interest is paid on overpayments unless refunded
within 90 days of the later of the final filing date or the completed return's filing. (A): the
municipality's own 3-year limit on assessments and civil actions.
*Current version effective 2025-09-30 (HB 96, 136th GA).*

**Temporary overrides:** for tax years 2020–2021 these rules were displaced for remote work by
[HB 197 §29](hb-197-2020.md) and [HB 110](hb-110-2021.md) — see
[Work-from-home rules by year](../concepts/work-from-home-rules-by-year.md).
